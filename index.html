Welcome to Violet & Jack B&B

Traditional charming 16th Century farmhouse of high standard accommodation on a working Beef & Sheep Farm, attention to detail is a priority. Tastefully Decorated 3 En-suite Rooms with every comfort assured. Breakfast and Rose award 2016, AGA cooked full English breakfast using local produce when available. Easy to locate off the A390 only 2 miles from the Cornish border, ideal for country walks locally or the Dartmoor National Park. Perfect accommodation for relaxing the new Garden Room or Conservatory in the summer or by the large Wood Burner for cosy nights in. We have amble parking & wifi for guests to use. Come as Guests then leave as Friends.
