Dundee Room

Our Double Room With Common Bathroom



Enjoy A Well Earned Rest In Our Twin Rooms.






Our standard standard double rooms, but with two single beds in place of a double, making them ideal for friends or travelling companions. Help yourself to a full range of modern amenities, relax and enjoy a wonderful night’s sleep in our comfortable and spacious classic twin rooms.



What To Expect When You Stay



Room Specifications:There are 2 rooms of this specification at Jack & Violet b&b that are available to book, with a cosy feel that is perfect for a good nights rest whether you’re travelling solo or with a companion. These rooms can have a maximum number of 2 guests. The average room size is 17.75m2.



Room Details:Each room has 2 single beds each along with a seating / vanity area and chair, an alarm clock with docking station for your mobile devices, an in-room telephone and TV complete with the latest Freeview Channels as well as Wi-Fi access for browsing.



Location & View:Both rooms are located on the first floor and are accessible via the South stairs. One room has a view of our internal courtyard, while one has a view of village life.



En-Suite Facilities:Both rooms have an ensuite bathroom. One room has a shower and separate bath, while the other just has a walk-in shower.



Dog Friendly:All of our Guest Rooms are dog friendly and all dogs must be booked in prior to arrival. For more information, please take a look at our Dog Friendly Paws For Thought Policy!



Parking:However long you choose to stay at The Inn on the Moor Hotel, there is free on-site parking spaces to use.
